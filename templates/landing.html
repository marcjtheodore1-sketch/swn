Supported by Volunteers

Every stroll is always supported by at least two London Autism Group Charity volunteers. This ensures there is always someone available to help, answer questions, and provide assistance if needed. You're in safe hands throughout your walk.

Accessibility First

All our routes are designed to be wheelchair accessible and inclusive. We call them "strolls" because we want everyone to feel welcome — whether you walk, roll, or stroll at your own pace.
